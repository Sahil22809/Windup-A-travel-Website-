About DB city mall

This plush shopping mall is situated in Arera hills, Maharana Pratap Nagar area in Bhopal. It has a six-screen multiplex operated by Cinepolis. DB City mall is one of the best malls in Bhopal housing maximum number of domestic and international brands fashion stores such as Pantaloon, Nike, Addidas along with individual shops. There is a food court with numerous chain outlets eating joints and fine dining. The huge mall offers awesome shopping, dining and entertainment experience, and comfortable parking lot as well. The place fulfills all requirements including groceries and clothing.
