About Peoples mall

People's Mall is a water park it is really a great place to do a lot. It has beautiful replicas of 7 wonders, water parks, go karting, games, multiplexes, malls etc. The place offers some of the best eateries in the city and several shops. This is a great and fun place for kids. This place is a must visit with your kids.
