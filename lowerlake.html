About Lower Lake

Lower lake in Bhopal offers a truly magnificent sight as you will be mesmerized by its picturesque beauty. It is popularly known as Chhota Talab. The Upper and Lower lakes are separated by an over-bridge called Lower Lake Bridge or Pul Pukhta. Enjoy boating on the serene waters with a breathtaking view of scenic beauty with gaggle geese around as the cool breeze softly caresses you. Indulge in thrilling boat rides, romantic paddle rides, and other adventure activities such as jet skiing and parasailing from the Boat Club.
